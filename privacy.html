We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Data transmission using SSL/TLS encryption

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website performance and functionality

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices and settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

When we no longer need your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
